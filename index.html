At Ciudad Retoño

We face an urgent challenge: the decrease of pollinators and inequality in access to green spaces. Through the Nektli project we seek to reverse this situation by creating the Retoñando Vida program. This program offers pollinator and food gardens for both bees and the community, promoting social and environmental justice.

Data-based decision-making

We’ve put together an open source solution that monitors temperature, light, moisture, weight and bee counting to enhance both gardening and beekeeping with data-based decision-making. Our goal is to turn data into knowledge that can transform into value for you or your company.

Social Impact: Sowing hope

SDGs, EDUCATION-RELATED ACTIVITIES "Jardines Retoñando Vida" revitalizes urban biodiversity, creating oases for pollinators and fostering a connection with nature. By adopting this replicable model, we contribute to SDG 4: Quality Education (11), Sustainable Cities and Communities (13), Climate Action (15), Life on Land, creating inclusive and resilient green spaces, and Partnerships for the Goals (17). This initiative combines environmental conservation with education and citizen participation, offering a unique value proposition for both ecosystems and those who wish to contribute.
