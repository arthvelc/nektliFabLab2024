At Ciudad Retoño

We face an urgent challenge: the decrease of pollinators and inequality in access to green spaces. Through the Nektli project we seek to reverse this situation by creating the RetoÑando Vida program. This program offers pollinator and food gardens for both bees and the community, promoting social and environmental justice.

Our project focuses on species and ecosystem diversity

Thinking globally & Acting locally

Thinking globally & Acting locally by removing concrete. Its benefits are immediate: reducing the heat island effect and allowing water availability!
